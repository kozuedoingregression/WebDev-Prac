Understanding HTML5 Tags

HTML5 introduces many new tags that help structure content in a more meaningful way. Here are a few:

  • Video: Embeds video content.
  • Audio: Embeds audio content.
  • Embed: Embeds external content, such as PDFs.
  • Article: Defines a section of content that forms an independent part of a document or site.
